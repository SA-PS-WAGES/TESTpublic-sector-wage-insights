üîÅ How membership dues and capitation work

Members pay dues as a percentage of wages. That money is split:

  • Some is sent upstream as capitation.

Important clarification:

  • PSA pays capitation out of the dues members already pay.

The incentive structure is simple:

  • More members bring in money straight away.
  • Higher wages bring in more money only if the fight is won.
